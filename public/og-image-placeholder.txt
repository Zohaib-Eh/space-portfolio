OG Image Placeholder
====================

Replace this file with a real `public/og-image.png` (1200 × 630 px) before deploying.

The og-image.png is referenced in app/layout.tsx under openGraph.images and will be
shown when your portfolio link is shared on social media (Twitter/X, LinkedIn, Discord, etc.).

Recommended tools for creating an OG image:
- Figma (export at 1200x630)
- og-image.vercel.app
- satori (programmatic via Next.js /api/og route)
- Canva (custom dimensions: 1200 x 630)

Once you have the image, place it at: public/og-image.png
